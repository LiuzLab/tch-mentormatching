overview

My research focuses on expanding access to effective behavioral interventions
to help people thrive while living with chronic diseases and health conditions
(e.g., diabetes, chronic pain). A primary research objective is to develop
scalable interventions that help reduce health disparities and improve
outcomes for diverse and underserved communities.
