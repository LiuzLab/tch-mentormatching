overview

I am a Professor of Pediatrics and a Neonatologist at Baylor College of
Medicine, Houston, TX. I have had the opportunity to train in India, United
Kingdom and the US. My goal is to advance the paradigm of precision medicine
in Neonatology via the use of multiomics, clinical data and machine learning
to derive predictive models and identify biomarkers for mortality and
morbidity in very preterm infants. My earlier research both translational and
clinical, focused on neonatal infections and the developing microbiome in
preterm infants. I am passionate about advancing evidence based medicine and
evidence based practice in Pediatrics.
