overview

Dr. Rana is an accomplished outcomes researcher with over 20 published
articles in esteemed journals. He has an expertise in liver and kidney
transplantation as well as surgeries for malignant and non-malignant
conditions that affect the liver, gallbladder, and bile ducts.
