![Collapse](https://profiles.viictr.org/Profile/Modules/PropertyList/images/minusSign.gif)
overview

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Medicine, Section of
Gastroenterology and Hepatology, with secondary appointments in the Menninger
Department of Psychiatry and Behavioral Sciences and the Section of Health
Services Research. I joined the Baylor faculty in 2016 and was tasked with
developing and implementing a psychology service, embedded in the Baylor
Gastroenterology Clinic, for patients with digestive diseases and other
serious and chronic medical conditions. My clinical practice focuses on the
integration of psychological services within medical care settings.  
  
My research interests include psychological aspects of medical illness and
dissemination and implementation in the area of patient safety. As a member of
the Center for Innovations in Quality, Effectiveness and Safety, I am active
in team science activities in the latter area.
