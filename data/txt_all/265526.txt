overview

I am a physician-scientist and a medical educator in pediatrics. My research
focuses in the development of patient-derived tumor xenografts of pediatric
cancers, particularly sarcomas. My research has been supported by the American
Society of Clinical Oncology, the Cancer Prevention & Research Institute of
Texas and several private foundations. I have served as reviewer in many
journals and grant review panels.  
I am also interested in studying the deleterious cardiovascular effects of
cancer therapy in pediatric patients. I am currently the Co-Director of the
Cardio-oncology Program at Texas Children's Hospital. I have several
publications and invited presentations related to this topic.  
My interest in medical education includes developing enduring education
materials and promoting self-reflection as a tool for professional growth in
trainees and faculty. I am member of the PREP Hematology/Oncology Editorial
Board of the American Academy of Pediatrics, and the Academy of Distinguished
Educators at Baylor College of Medicine.
