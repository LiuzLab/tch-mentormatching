I01 HX00312 (Nader Massarweh)May 1, 2021 - Apr 30, 2025VA HSR&D  
Enhancing the Efficiency of Data Collection for Quality Improvement and
Research  
Role Description: To evaluate the reliability of VASQIP’s current case
sampling strategy for identifying outlier VA hospitals, explore the use of
hybrid data as a means of enhancing data collection efficiency, and will
identify how more efficient data collection could enhance local QI through
semi-structured interviews with Surgical Quality Nurses.  
Role: Co-Investigator  
  
* * *  
  
I01 HX002917 (Salim Virani & Sankar Navaneethan)Jul 1, 2020 - Jun 30, 2023VA
HSR&D  
Optimizing Renin Angiotensin System Blocker Use among Veterans with Kidney
Disease  
Role Description: This study aims to understand the reasons for underuse of
ACEI/ARB attributed to side effects versus clinical inertia using structured
datasets and unstructured provider text notes, and barriers to initiation and
re-initiation (after being discontinued) of ACEI/ARB.  
Role: Statistician
