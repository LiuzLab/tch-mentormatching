overview

Dr. Allen L. Milewicz has extensive experience and expertise in the spectrum
of pediatric surgery. He has specialized training in hepatobiliary disorders
and skeletal malformations of the chest and research training in liver
transplant and cardiac surgery. Dr. Milewicz is Board Certified in Surgery and
in General Surgery.
