overview

Dr. Konstantinos (Kostas) Makris graduated cum laude from the University of
Athens Medical School in Greece. He received his General Surgery training at
the Mayo Clinic in Rochester, MN and Creighton University Medical Center in
Omaha, NE and has been certified by the American Board of Surgery. He pursued
fellowship training in Advanced Laparoscopy and Surgical Endoscopy at Legacy
Health in Portland, OR, as well as further training in Endocrine Surgery at
Johns Hopkins University in Baltimore, MD. He has received awards and
acknowledgements by the American College of Surgeons for his participation in
the contest of surgical knowledge and in the competition of surgical trainee
essay, as well as a traveling fellowship award by the Society of American
Gastrointestinal and Endoscopic Surgeons.
