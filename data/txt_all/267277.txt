overview

Dr. Charles H. McCollum joined the Department as faculty member in 1966 and
has served as acting chair of the Department, as well as interim chief of both
the Division of Vascular Surgery & Endovascular Therapy and the Division of
Cardiothoracic Surgery. Dr. McCollum ran general surgery as program director
for 23 years. His research focused on non-invasive studies for the diagnosis
of peripheral vascular disease using ultra sound technology.
