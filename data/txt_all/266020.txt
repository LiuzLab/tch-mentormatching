overview

Dr. Blumenthal-Barby's research focuses on the ethical issues raised by
research on human judgment and decision-making (e.g., decisional biases and
heuristics). She focuses on developing moral guidelines for the use of
principles from behavioral economics and decisional psychology (e.g., framing
effects, default options, incentives, focusing effects, commitment contracts,
and subconscious priming) to shape peopleâ€™s medical decisions and behaviors.
