overview

Dr. Norman L. Sussman is leading an effort to improve medical resources in
rural and underserved communities in Texas using a videoconference outreach
model. He is also continuing to refine methods of assessing risk in patient
with acute liver failure.  
  
Dr. Sussman is an active member of the Baylor - St. Luke’s Liver Center. He is
Board Certified in Internal Medicine, Gastroenterology, and Transplant
Hepatology. His clinical interests cover all aspects of liver disease, and his
research interests include assessment and management of acute liver failure,
artificial liver support, new agents to treat viral hepatitis, and
complications of advanced cirrhosis.
