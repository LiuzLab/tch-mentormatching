overview

Dr. Paul K. Minifee specializes in general surgical treatments and procedures
for infants and children. He is committed to providing the finest patient-
oriented pediatric surgical care. This philosophy combines dedication to
exceptional education and training with development of newer, better
treatments and procedures. Dr. Minifee is Board Certified in Surgery and in
Pediatric Surgery.  
  
Dr. Minifee, primarily a clinical pediatric surgeon, has a commitment to
education. He routinely provides high school and medical school mentorship
through programs such as the High School Mentorship Program at Texas
Childrenâ€™s Hospital, the Honors Premedical Academy, and the Longitudinal
Ambulatory Clinical Experience (LACE) course at Baylor College of Medicine.
Dr. Minifee combines education and technology as he mentors Baylor residents
and medical students on clinical rotations in pediatric surgery.
