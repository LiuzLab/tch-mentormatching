overview

Dr. Betty del Rio Rodriguez, MD, MPH is Assistant Professor of Pediatrics with
Baylor College of Medicine, and member of the Section of Academic General
Pediatrics at Texas Childrenâ€™s Hospital. She is a board certified pediatrician
and since 2013 has worked as an attending physician at the Pasadena Pediatric
and Adolescent Health Center. Dr. Del Rio Rodriguez works closely with
Academic General Pediatrics fellows, pediatric residents and medical students
and oversights their learning experience at the clinical site. In 2023, she
became the Medical Director for the Pasadena Pediatric and Adolescent Health
Center with Harris Health System and seeks to advocate for pediatric patients
. Her areas of interest are underserved populations, mental health, parenting
and telemedicine.
