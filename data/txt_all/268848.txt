overview

Dr. Gilani’s research interest is in the clinical outcome of vascular surgical
reconstructions and endovascular interventions, specifically in endovascular
treatment of aortic aneurysms, venous disease, and endovascular treatment of
lower extremity occlusive disease.  
  
Dr. Gilani has contributed numerous articles to scholarly and professional
journals such as Journal of Vascular Surgery, Vascular and Endovascular
Surgery, Vascular Journal, and Journal of Endovascular Therapy. He has written
many book chapters related to vascular disease management.
