Overview  

![Collapse](https://profiles.viictr.org/Profile/Modules/PropertyList/images/minusSign.gif)
overview

Dr. Flores provides care for patients with congenital and acquired heart
disease requiring critical care at Texas Children's Hospital. Dr. Flores'
research focuses on understanding stress response mechanisms after
cardiopulmonary bypass for cardiac surgery in children.
