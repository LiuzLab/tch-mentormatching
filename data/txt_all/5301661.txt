overview

Dr. Peter Jindra's research interests are the role of anti-HLA antibodies in
transplant rejection, the effect of single nucleotide polymorphisms on bone
marrow transplant outcomes, the expression and function of microRNA in
lymphocytes and murine models of transplantation.
