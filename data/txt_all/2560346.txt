overview

In the Regulatory Circuits Lab (https://www.bcm.edu/research/labs/regulatory-
circuits) we infer pathophysiological consequences of genomic variants and
aberrant gene expression through analyses of molecular profiles. Towards this
goal we combine computational analyses and biochemical experiments, leveraging
regulatory circuits to identify new driver genes and interpret the function of
genomic variants that act in cis and in trans.
