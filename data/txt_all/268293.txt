overview

Being board certified in infectious diseases, Dr. Correa’s interests are in
the areas of immunizations, vaccine safety and periodic fever syndromes. He
provides independent safety monitoring for clinical trials involving newer
vaccines, such as H1N1 (2009) influenza and rotavirus.  
  
Being board certified in infectious diseases, Dr. Correa’s interests are in
the areas of immunizations, vaccine safety and periodic fever syndromes. He
provides independent safety monitoring for clinical trials involving newer
vaccines, such as H1N1 (2009) influenza and rotavirus. He is also
collaborating in a study to validate a new biomarker of β cell death in
pediatric type 1 diabetes.
