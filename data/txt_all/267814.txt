overview

Matthew J. Wall Jr., is committed to providing superior, patient-focused care
and preparing the next generation of surgeons to meet the highest standards of
excellence. This vision combines a dedication to advanced research,
exceptional education, and the development of newer, better treatments and
procedures.  
  
Dr. Wall participated in the original Baylor Master Teacher Fellowship; he
lectures at BCM, and in other national and international forums.  
  
His research interests include the management of the injured patient, novel
resuscitation strategies, and injures to the chest and the vascular system.
