![Collapse](https://profiles.viictr.org/Profile/Modules/PropertyList/images/minusSign.gif)
overview

I am a pediatrician with backgrounds in Pediatric Critical Care Medicine and
Palliative Care. I assist in the management of hospitalized children of all
ages and their families at Texas Children's Hospital. My clinical interests
are acute and palliative care for children throughout the world. I am very
fortunate to work with exceptional professionals at my home institution, and
to participate in international collaborative efforts in acute care and
palliative care education and practice, primarily through the Society of
Critical Care Medicine and the Latin American Society of Palliative Care.
