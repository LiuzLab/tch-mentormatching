overview

Shubhada Hooli, MD, MPH is a pediatric emergency medicine physician who
conducts research to reduce child pneumonia related-mortality globally. She
transitioned to a career focused on research after living in SubSaharan Africa
for three years working as a clinician-educator. She thrives in equitable
collaborative partnerships and explores how innovations in technology could
change the way we practice medicine.
