overview

Jeanine Graf is a Professor in Pediatric Intensive Care. Research interests
include long term outcomes with pediatric tracheostomies, as well as pediatric
transport medicine. Dr. Graf is Chief Medical Officer at Texas Children's
Hospital West Campus located in West Houston.
