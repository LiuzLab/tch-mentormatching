overview

Dr. Christine A. O'Mahony has research interest in clinical outcomes -
specifically in kidney and liver transplant patients.  
  
Dr. O'Mahony has been a Baylor College of Medicine faculty member since 2004.
She specializes in adult and pediatric liver transplantation, hepatobiliary
conditions, and the surgical management of liver tumors.
